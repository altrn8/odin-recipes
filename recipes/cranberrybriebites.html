Cranberry Brie Bites

Description

Tangy, Creamy and Crunchy appetizer that combines cranberry, brie cheese and walnuts in a puff pastry shell!

Ingredients

  • Brie
  • Puff Pastry
  • Cranberry Sauce
  • Walnuts
  • Sea Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Roll the puff pastry into a rectangle
  3. Place Brie in freezer for about 20 min. Meanwhile, cut the pastry into even squares. Remove Brie from freezer and cut into even pieces.
  4. Press pastry squares into an oiled muffin tin. Spoon each square with a teaspoon of cranberry sauce, then top with the Brie. Sprinkle with walnuts and a pinch of sea salt.
  5. Transfer cranberry brie bites to fridge and chill for about 30 min
  6. Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown
