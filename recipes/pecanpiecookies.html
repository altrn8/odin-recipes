Pecan Pie Cookies

Description

Perfectly round and sweet treat for a Thanksgiving desert or a fall get-together. These Pecan Pie cookies are going to steal the spotlight during the Holiday's from the turkey!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Corn Syrup
  • Pecans
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Egg
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Cook the first three ingredients over medium heat until sugar dissolves.
  2. Stir in pecans.
  3. Beat wet ingredients for the dough together, gradually add dry mixture.
  4. Roll dough into balls and press into muffin cups. Fill with pecan filling.
  5. bake until edges are lightly brown.
