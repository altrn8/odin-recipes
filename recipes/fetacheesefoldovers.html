Feta Cheese Foldovers

Description

Fluffy and savory appetizer to keep guests busy before the Thanksgiving feast!

Ingredients

  • Feta Cheese
  • Green Onion
  • Egg
  • Frozen puff pastry
  • Egg yolk w/ 1 teaspoon water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Blend feta, green onion and egg in small bowl, cut pastry into 12 3 inch squares. Place tablespoon of feta mixture in center of each square. Moisten edges with water, fold pastry over the filling to form triangle. Press edges together firmly with a fork to seal. Lightly brush pastries with egg yolk mixture.
  3. Bake for 20 min or until golden brown. Serve warm or at room temperature.
